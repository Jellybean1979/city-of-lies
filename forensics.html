Laboratory Divisions

Forensic DNA Lab CODIS-linked · Biological ID
Available

Examines biological samples — blood, hair, saliva, and skin cells — for genetic identification. Profiles are cross-referenced against CODIS to identify known offenders or link scenes.

Blood typing & full DNA profiling Hair follicle mitochondrial analysis Saliva & touch DNA recovery Scene-to-suspect genetic linking
Database: CODIS
🧪
Forensic Toxicology Lab Substance Detection · Fluids & Tissue
Available

Analyses bodily fluids and tissue samples to identify the presence of drugs, alcohol, prescription medications, or poisons. Used in both cause-of-death and victim impairment investigations.

Blood alcohol & narcotics screening Prescription drug identification Poison & heavy metal detection Post-mortem toxicological panel
🔍
Trace Evidence Lab Micro-material Analysis
Available

Analyses tiny materials transferred between people, objects, and environments. Locard's Exchange Principle: every contact leaves a trace. Fibres, glass, soil, paint, and fire debris are examined to link suspects to scenes.

Fibre origin & textile matching Glass fracture & composition analysis Soil & botanical comparison Paint chip layering & vehicle matching Fire debris accelerant detection
🔫
Ballistics & Firearms Lab NIBIN-linked · Weapons Analysis
Available

Examines firearms, bullets, cartridge casings, and gunshot residue. Firing patterns are matched against NIBIN to link weapons across multiple incidents or identify unregistered firearms.

Bullet & casing ballistic comparison Gunshot residue analysis (GSR) Weapon condition & origin tracing Entry/exit wound trajectory analysis
Database: NIBIN
💻
Digital & Multimedia Lab Digital Forensics · Data Recovery
In Use

Recovers and analyses data from computers, smartphones, USB drives, and surveillance systems. Deleted files, encrypted communications, and metadata are all within scope.

Mobile device imaging & extraction Deleted file recovery & carving CCTV enhancement & frame analysis Encrypted communication decryption Metadata & geolocation extraction
⚗️
Forensic Chemistry Lab Drug Analysis · Arson Investigation
Available

Identifies unknown substances and analyses arson evidence. Controlled substance profiling, cutting agent identification, and detection of ignition accelerants in fire debris all fall under this division.

Controlled substance identification & purity Cutting agent & adulterant profiling Ignition accelerant detection (arson) Unknown liquid & powder composition
👆
Latent Print Lab AFIS-linked · Friction Ridge Analysis
Available

Processes, develops, and analyses fingerprints and palm prints recovered from crime scenes and physical evidence. Identified prints are compared against AFIS for positive identification.

Latent print development (powder, chemical) Fingerprint & palmprint comparison Partial print enhancement & matching AFIS database search & cold hit reporting
Database: AFIS
📝
Document Analysis Lab Questioned Documents · Handwriting
Available

Examines handwriting, ink composition, paper origin, and document alteration. Used to authenticate or challenge the origin of written evidence, identify forgeries, and analyse anonymous communications.

Handwriting comparison & author attribution Ink dating & composition analysis Paper fibre & watermark identification Typewriter font & printer matching Erasure, alteration & forgery detection
🦴
Forensic Anthropology Lab Human Remains · Biological Evidence
Available

Identifies human remains and analyses biological scene evidence. Also covers forensic entomology (insect activity to estimate time of death) and forensic botany (plant material as evidence).

Skeletal identification — age, sex, ancestry Trauma pattern analysis on bone Insect colonisation — post-mortem interval Plant material & pollen evidence analysis
☣️
Biocontainment Lab NBFAC · Hazardous Biological Evidence
Restricted

Specialises in the analysis of hazardous or contaminated biological evidence under strict containment protocols. Handles potential pathogens, biological weapons components, and high-risk materials. Access requires special clearance.

Pathogen identification & containment Biological agent characterisation Contaminated evidence processing Chain-of-custody under BSL-3/4 protocol
⚙️
Metallurgy Lab Metal Composition · Structural Analysis
Available

Analyses metal evidence to investigate structural failures, weapon composition, and tool marks. Can identify the origin and manufacture of metal objects and determine whether tampering or deliberate failure occurred.

Elemental composition & alloy identification Tool mark comparison & origin Fracture & stress failure analysis Weapon manufacture & modification tracing
